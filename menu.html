Savory Samurai Menu

Menu

Appetizers:

  • The Art of Takoyaki ($4.49) - A lovely batch of delight. Batter stuffed with minced octopus.
  • Ninja Gyoza ($4.99) - A simple mix of minced pork, cabbage, and mushroom, wrapped with thin dough.
  • Swordsman's Harumaki ($3.99) - A crispy Japanese spring roll stuffed with pork, carrots, bamboo shoots, mushrooms, and vermicelli noodles.

Main Courses:

  • Shogun's Sushi ($6.49) - An outstanding mix of rice, seafood, and vegetables wrapped together with seaweed.
  • Ronin's Ramen ($6.79) - A popular choice. You can get it with chicken, shrimp, and/or beef.
  • Daimyo's Udon ($6.99) - Another kind of amazing and savory noodle soup that's slightly less well-known as ramen, but is definitely worth a try.

Desserts:

  • Sun Tzu's Purin ($4.99) - A sweet, creamy treat that's similar to flan.
  • Mighty Mille Crepe Cake ($29.99) - A magnificent layered cake made of crepe and cream.
  • Mentor's Mizu Yokan ($6.79) - A Japanese red bean jelly that contains sweetened chestnuts and has been chilled.

Beverages:

  • Wisdom Green Tea ($3.59) - A simple, but delightful tea that uses leaves from the Camellia sinensis plant.
  • Brawly Bubble Tea ($5.79) - A favorite of many. Tapioca pearls are dropped into a mix of tea, milk, and fruit.
  • Filthy Rich Royal Milk Tea ($7.49) - A creamy beverage that uses milk to simmer black tea instead of boiling water.
