Sonic X


Show Info

Main Characters

  • Sonic The Hedgehog
  • Chris Thorndyke

Plot

Sonic, his friends, and Doctor Robotnik are teleported to Earth due to an accidental
Chaos Control. We meet Chris when he later saves Sonic from drowning in his pool and
they become friends. Sonic's other friends now want to retrieve all the chaos emeralds to
use them to get back to their world, but Doctor Robotnik wants them for himself to take
over Earth. Sonic isn't in as much of a rush to get back and would rather explore Earth.

Personal Thoughts

I believe that this show was pretty good and I had fun watching it. The story was
pretty well written and the characters were all enjoyable. It didn't seem too far off
from the sonic lore and the show as a whole had its own unique charm to it.


Character Details

Sonic the Hedgehog
A cool and funny daredevil that cares about his friends and will do what he
can to protect them. He loves chilidogs and long runs. He'll also travel around the world to
admire all the different sights there are to see. He likes making new friends and has been
enemies with Doctor Robotnik for a long time.
Chris Thorndyke
A calm human boy that befriends Sonic after saving him and getting to know
him better. He's well-mannered, but also enjoys a bit of adventure. Sometimes he gets
into a bit of trouble when going on adventures, though.
Tails (Miles Prower)
A smart and kind tech-savvy who is Sonic's best friend. He makes a lot of inventions and even
has his own airplane called the Tornado. The difference between his machines and Dr. Robotniks'
is that Tails uses them for good. On a side note, Tails can even use his twin tails like propellors,
so that he can fly.
Knuckles
An obstinate and short tempered echidna who's the last of an ancient tribe of
other echidnas. He guards the Master Emerald, which is a giant mystical emerald
that has the power to control the seven chaos emeralds. Knuckles believes that everyone
has good in them and can change, but that also leads to him being very naive.
Doctor Robotnik
An evil mastermind that's very power hungry and will use his inventions to
gain that power. He'll also try to manipulate others, such as Knuckles,
to get them on his side to have even more control. He's the main antagonist.
Shadow the Hedgehog
A cold and aggressive lonewolf that's not very social and doesn't let anyone or anything stand in
his way. He believes that he is the "Ultimate Lifeform" and tends to see others as being beneathe
him, but he does have people that he does truly care about like Maria and Rouge.
