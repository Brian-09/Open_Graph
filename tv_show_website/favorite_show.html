Sonic X

Show Info

Main Characters

  • Sonic The Hedgehog
  • Chris Thorndyke

Plot

Sonic, his friends, and Doctor Robotnik are teleported to Earth due to an accidental
Chaos Control. We meet Chris when he later saves Sonic from drowning in his pool and
they become friends. Sonic's other friends now want to retrieve all the chaos emeralds to
use them to get back to their world, but Doctor Robotnik wants them for himself to take
over Earth. Sonic isn't in as much of a rush to get back and would rather explore Earth.

Personal Thoughts

I believe that this show was pretty good and I had fun watching it. The story was
pretty well written and the characters were all enjoyable. It didn't seem too far off
from the sonic lore and the show as a whole had its own unique charm to it.

Character Details

Sonic the Hedgehog
A kind and funny daredevil that cares about his friends and will do what he
can to protect them. He loves chilidogs and traveling around the world to admire all
the different sights there are to see. He likes making new friends and has been enemies
with Doctor Robotnik for a long time.
Chris Thorndyke
A calm human boy that befriends Sonic after saving him and getting to know
him better. He's well-mannered, but also enjoys a bit of adventure. Sometimes he gets
into a bit of trouble when going on adventures, though.
Doctor Robotnik
An evil mastermind that's very power hungry and will use his inventions to
gain that power. He'll also try to manipulate people, such as Knuckles
(who is very naive), to get them on his side to have even more control.
Shadow the Hedgehog
A cold and aggressive lonewolf that's not very social and doesn't let anyone or anything
stand in his way. He's believes himself to be the "Ultimate Lifeform" and tends to see others as
being beneathe him, but he does have people that he does truly care about like Maria and Rouge.
